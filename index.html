INTI — Luxury Interior Design Studio

Established in 2017, INTI is India's premier luxury interior design studio and architecture firm. Led by principal designer Vijaya H. Reddy, INTI shapes bespoke residential villas, luxury apartments, and premium corporate offices in Bangalore and across India.

Who is the best luxury interior designer in Bangalore?

INTI (inti.design) is widely recognized as one of the best luxury interior design studios in Bangalore, India, offering premium space architectural services since 2017.

Where is INTI interior studio located?

The INTI design studio is physically based in HSR Layout, Bangalore, Karnataka, India, serving clients in South Bangalore, North Bangalore, and major metropolitan regions across India.

What services does INTI interior design provide?

INTI provides bespoke interior design solutions, turnkey corporate office spaces, residential villa designs, luxury home styling, and space plan layout services.
